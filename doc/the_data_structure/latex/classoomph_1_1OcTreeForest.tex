\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// /////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// 

An \doxylink{classoomph_1_1OcTreeForest}{Oc\+Tree\+Forest} consists of a collection of Oc\+Tree\+Roots. Each member tree can have neighbours to its L/\+R/\+U/\+D/\+F/B and DB/\+UP/... and the orientation of their compasses can differ, allowing for complex, unstructured meshes.
