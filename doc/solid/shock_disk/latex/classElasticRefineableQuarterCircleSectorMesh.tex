//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// //////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////// 

Elastic quarter circle sector mesh with functionality to attach traction elements to the curved surface. We "{}upgrade"{} the Refineable\+Quarter\+Circle\+Sector\+Mesh to become an Solid\+Mesh and equate the Eulerian and Lagrangian coordinates, thus making the domain represented by the mesh the stress-\/free configuration. ~\newline
~\newline
